SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE - TEAM STREET PRECISION

1. SENSING LAYER: 
   - Raspberry Pi Camera (CSI Connection): Captures 720p video feed for lane detection.

2. PROCESSING LAYER (High-Level):
   - Raspberry Pi 4 (8GB): Runs the main Python environment, handles image processing, 
     and hosts the Web Dashboard.

3. CONTROL LAYER (Low-Level):
   - STM32 Nucleo Board: Receives serial commands from the Pi to control 
     the Steering Servo and DC Motor (Velocity).

4. CONNECTIVITY:
   - Wi-Fi (SSH/TCP): Connects the car to the PC for manual control and monitoring.
